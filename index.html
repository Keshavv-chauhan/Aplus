Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about APlus Learning Institute

1. What is APlus Learning Institute?

APlus Learning Institute is an online platform offering personalized tutoring and courses in academics, languages, computer science, and competitive exams for students worldwide.

2. How do I book a free demo class?

You can book a free 30-minute demo class by filling out the contact form and selecting "Yes" for the demo class option.

3. What boards and curricula do you cover?

We cover CBSE, ICSE, State Boards of India, IB, IGCSE, AQA, MYP, A Level, AP, and more.

4. Are your courses available internationally?

Yes, our courses are available to students worldwide, and we have learners from India, UAE, USA, UK, Canada, and more.

5. What platforms do you use for online classes?

We use Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and WhatsApp for live classes and communication.

6. Who are the instructors?

Our instructors are experienced, qualified teachers and professionals from India, specializing in their respective subjects.

7. Can I choose my own schedule?

Yes, we offer flexible scheduling to accommodate students in different time zones and with varying availability.

8. How do I pay for the courses?

Payment options and details will be shared after registration. We support multiple payment methods for international students.

9. Do you provide study materials?

Yes, we provide digital study materials, assignments, quizzes, and resources through Google Classroom and other platforms.

10. How can I contact support?

You can reach us via the Contact Us form on this website or email us at info@apluslearning.com.

About APlus Learning Institute

Empowering learners worldwide with quality education

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and accessible online learning platform for students worldwide by delivering high-quality, personalized education from India, bridging borders with knowledge and technology.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 and established in 2024, APlus Learning Institute has been at the forefront of online education, providing high-quality courses to students worldwide. Our mission is to make quality education accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or background.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver a well-rounded and accessible online learning experience for school students, college learners, and professionals in Dubai by:
Providing expert-led tutoring in core academic subjects (Math, Science, English, Social Studies) aligned with CBSE, ICSE, British, IB, and American curricula.
Offering specialized, hands-on courses in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Python, coding, and project-based tech learning.
Promoting cultural identity by teaching Indian languages like Hindi, Punjabi, and Sanskrit to students of Indian origin.
Enabling global communication skills through foreign language programs in German, Spanish, and Japanese, taught by certified language instructors.
Utilizing digital tools, live interactive sessions, personalized mentorship, and performance tracking to create a seamless and engaging online learning journey.
Operating from India, leveraging the country's educational talent to deliver cost-effective, high-quality tutoring
